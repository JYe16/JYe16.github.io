Zhankai Ye

Ph.D. Student in Computer Science

Florida State University

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student at Florida State University. My research lies at the intersection of security, privacy, and ubiquitous AI. I focus on developing trustworthy radio frequency (RF) sensing systems and securing the deployment of large language models (LLMs) in physical environments. Specifically, I investigate privacy-preserving frameworks for multimodal sensing—aiming to protect sensitive human behavioral data from unauthorized inference—and design secure, resource-efficient architectures that guarantee data privacy during on-device execution at the edge.
